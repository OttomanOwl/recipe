Rocky Road

This recipe is a Christmas tradion. It's the perfect combonation of chocolate, marshmallows and peanuts.                                                                   They are a treat that the entire family will love this year and next. It's food. Just buy it.

Ingredients:

  • 1 10oz package of miniature marshmallows.
  • 1 Small can condensed milk.
  • 1 12oz package of chocolate chips.
  • 2 Tbsp butter.
  • 2 Cups of roasted unslated peanuts.

Method:

  1. Grease a pan and place wax paper on top.
  2. Melt butter, chocolate chips and condensed milk. In the top of a double boiler.
  3. Toss the marshmallows and nuts in a large bowl.
  4. When chocolate mixture has cooled slightly, pour it over the nuts and marshmallows, spread mixture evenly in a 13x9 pan. That has been lined and greased in wax paper.
  5. Put into fridge, for 2 hours to chill.
